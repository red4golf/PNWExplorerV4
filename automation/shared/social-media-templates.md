## Platform-Specific Optimizations

### Instagram
- **Character limit**: 2,200 (use 1,800-2,000 for safety)
- **Hashtag strategy**: 20-30 hashtags, mix of popular and niche
- **Best posting times**: 6-7 AM, 7-9 PM
- **Content focus**: Visual storytelling, behind-the-scenes

### Twitter
- **Character limit**: 280 (use 250-270 for safety)
- **Thread strategy**: Break long stories into 3-5 tweet threads
- **Best posting times**: 8-10 AM, 7-9 PM
- **Content focus**: Quick facts, breaking news, conversations

### Facebook
- **Character limit**: 63,206 (use 1,000-1,500 for optimal engagement)
- **Post strategy**: Longer, more detailed content
- **Best posting times**: 1-3 PM, 8-9 PM
- **Content focus**: Community building, detailed stories

### LinkedIn
- **Character limit**: 3,000 (use 1,500-2,000 for optimal engagement)
- **Post strategy**: Professional insights, business history
- **Best posting times**: 7-8 AM, 5-6 PM (weekdays)
- **Content focus**: Professional lessons, industry insights
